Open Salsa OG (garlic mayo)

Ingredients

  • 3 garlic cloves.

  • 1 lemon juice.

  • 1 egg.

  • 200 Gr. chopped cilantro (preferably without the stem).

  • 1 teaspoon of pepper.

  • 2 teaspoons of salt.

  • 250 Gr. of canola or olive oil.

Preparation

In the blender add: the coriander, the lemon juice, the egg, the garlic cloves, the salt and the pepper. Blend everything until the mixture is homogeneous. First step of the sauce, very simple.
The oil is what will make the sauce thick and get a mayonnaise like texture, so this is the most critical point of the preparation. Once we have the homogeneous mixture, turn on the blender again. While the blender rotates, in the center of the mixture a small hole will appear. Slowly pour the oil into this hole until the sauce reaches the desired texture.
Voilá, the salsa is lista.
(Due the egg the salsa should be consumed within 4 days before it goes not so good)



The amounts stipulated for some ingredients are just a reference. There are people that like their salsa a little bit more salty or more sour, maybe thicker or more liquid. I encourage you to stick to the base recipe at least once and then you can go and explore with the amounts and ingredients.
